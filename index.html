OUR HERITAGE

The Goddess of Nourishment

✿

Sri Annapoorneswri Sweets is named after Goddess Annapoorneswari — the divine mother of nourishment and abundance. In her name, we craft sweets that carry blessings of purity, love, and devotion.

Founded over two decades ago in the heart of Bengaluru, we have been the trusted sweet shop for thousands of families celebrating weddings, festivals, and everyday moments of joy.

"We do not just make sweets. We make memories, wrapped in devotion and tied with love."

Every recipe is traditional South Indian, passed through generations with care. We use only pure ingredients — no preservatives, no shortcuts, no compromises.
